<p><p>The GPS trailer tracker market is segmented into wired and wireless trackers. Wired GPS trailer trackers are directly connected to the trailer's electrical system, offering reliable power and consistent connectivity. They are generally more difficult to tamper with but require professional installation. On the other hand, wireless GPS trailer trackers operate on battery power and are easier to install, providing flexibility and portability. Wireless options are favored for their ease of use, although they may require regular battery maintenance.</p></p>
