<p><p>The Industrial Steam Peeler market is segmented into two main types: batch steam peelers and continuous steam peelers. Batch steam peelers operate by processing small, defined quantities of produce at a time, making them suitable for smaller operations or varied product types. In contrast, continuous steam peelers are designed for high-volume processing, allowing for a steady flow of products through the peeling system, enhancing efficiency in larger production settings. Both types cater to specific operational needs within the food processing industry.</p></p>
