<p><p>The Portable Air Cleaner System market encompasses various technologies designed to purify indoor air. HEPA filters capture small particles, effectively removing allergens and pollutants. Activated carbon filters adsorb odors and gases. Electrostatic precipitators use electrical charges to trap particles, while ionizers emit ions to help neutralize airborne contaminants. Ozone generators produce ozone to eliminate odors and harmful substances, though they require caution due to potential health risks. Other systems may include ultraviolet light or photocatalytic oxidation technologies, offering diverse air purification options.</p></p>
