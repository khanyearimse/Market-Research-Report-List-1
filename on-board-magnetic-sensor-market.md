<p><p>The on-board magnetic sensor market includes several types of sensors that detect magnetic fields for various applications. Hall Effect Sensors measure magnetic fields by generating a voltage in response to magnetic flux. Magnetoresistive Sensors change their resistance based on magnetic field strength, offering high sensitivity. SQUID Sensors, or Superconducting QUantum Interference Devices, operate at low temperatures and provide extremely precise magnetic measurements. Other types may include fluxgate sensors and integrated magnetic sensors, each serving unique functionalities in automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
