<p><p>The microwave circulator market includes passive, three-port, and four-port types, each serving distinct functions in signal routing. Passive circulators allow RF signals to flow in one direction, enhancing isolation between ports without amplifying signal strength. Three-port circulators effectively manage signal distribution in applications like radar and communication systems. Four-port circulators offer enhanced connectivity options for more complex systems, enabling a greater number of circuits to be interconnected. These variants cater to diverse industry needs, from telecommunications to aerospace.</p></p>
